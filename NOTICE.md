## Procedural models (internals)

The engine, transaxle, brakes, and other internal component models under
`public/models/components/` are **original procedural geometry** generated by this
project's pipeline (`npm run gen:components`, see `tools/gen/`). No third-party
asset is used for them.

## Reference imagery

Reference photos used to inform the procedural geometry were sourced from Wikimedia
Commons (various CC licenses) and are kept only as build-time references under
`tools/gen/refs/` — they are not redistributed in the app.
